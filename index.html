Rules Snapshot

  • Start with one pawn each: White on c5, Black on f5.
  • Each turn: either move one on-board piece or place one reserve piece.
  • Placement is legal on empty non-town hexes only.
  • Reserve limits per player: 5 pawns, 2 horses, 2 sentinels, 1 teacher.
  • Most pieces move one adjacent hex.
  • Horses move one or two hexes in a straight line.
  • Pawns adjacent to a commander may hop over one friendly adjacent piece.
  • You win by controlling both towns OR eliminating all enemy pieces.
