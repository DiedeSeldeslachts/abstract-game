Abstract Capture Game

Kingstep

Modified chess setup with two commanders per side. Most pieces advance like a king, commanders can move up to two adjacent steps, and victory only comes from wiping out the opposing army.

Rules Snapshot

  • Back rank uses two commanders in the center files.
  • Most pieces move one square in any direction.
  • Commanders may move one or two adjacent king-style steps.
  • You capture by moving onto an enemy piece.
  • You only win after the opponent has zero pieces left.
