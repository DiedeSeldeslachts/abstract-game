Abstract Capture Game

Kingstep

Modified chess setup with two commanders per side. Most pieces advance like a king, while horses on files a and h move one or two squares in a straight line. Pawns adjacent to commanders can hop over friendly pieces. Win by eliminating all opponents OR controlling both towns on the extended ninth rank.

Rules Snapshot

  • Back rank uses horses on files a and h, commanders on c and f.
  • Most pieces move one square in any direction.
  • Horses move one or two squares in a straight line.
  • Pawns adjacent to a commander may hop over one friendly adjacent piece.
  • You capture by moving onto an enemy piece.
  • You win by controlling both towns OR eliminating all enemy pieces.
